Welcome To Hamilton Heights

Native New Yorkers and seasoned Manhattan bar veterans Brian Felicetta and Matthew Amsterdam bring their #farmtoharlem cocktail and fare to Hamilton Heights. Combining farm fresh produce and protiens with an inventive and immersive seasonal cocktail list. Choose craft beers from the five borroughs and surrounding New York State. Offering neighborhood appeal with an unpretentious elegance.
